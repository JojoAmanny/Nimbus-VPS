Our story

Built for developers,
by developers

We started NimbusVPS because we were tired of overpriced, overcomplicated cloud infrastructure that required a PhD to configure. So we built something better.

Who we are

Cloud infrastructure reimagined

Founded in 2021, NimbusVPS has grown from a small passion project into a trusted infrastructure provider serving over 40,000 servers worldwide. We believe that powerful cloud computing should be accessible to everyone — from indie hackers to enterprise teams.

Our mission is simple: deliver the most reliable, performant, and affordable VPS hosting on the planet, wrapped in an experience so clean and intuitive that deploying a server feels like magic.

Every decision we make — from the hardware we choose to the UI we design — is driven by one question: would a developer love this?

Our values

What we stand for

The principles that guide every decision we make.

⚡ Performance First
We obsess over every millisecond. From hardware selection to network routing, performance is never an afterthought — it's our foundation.
🔒 Relentless Reliability
Downtime costs money and trust. We engineer for 99.9% uptime, maintain N+1 redundancy across all systems, and monitor 24/7.
💚 Developer Experience
Good infrastructure should get out of your way. We design every feature, every API, every dashboard screen to delight the developer using it.
💰 Transparent Pricing
No surprise bills. No confusing tiers. No asterisks hiding fees. What you see on our pricing page is exactly what you pay.
🌱 Sustainability
We power our data centres with 100% renewable energy and are committed to carbon-neutral operations across our entire infrastructure.
🤝 Community
We give back to the open-source projects we depend on. We sponsor developers, contribute code, and support the ecosystem that made us possible.
